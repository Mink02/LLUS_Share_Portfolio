About Us

Fridays for Future is a global movement that began in August 2018, after 15-year-old Greta Thunberg and other young activists sat in front of the Swedish parliament every school day for three weeks to protest against the lack of action on the climate crisis.

Our Actions

We organize and participate in climate strikes and actions worldwide to demand action from our leaders.
